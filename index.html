Featured Projects
Satu Hati Mini Farm and Resort Website

The Satu Hati Mini Farm and Resort website seeks to modernize this picturesque vacation spot for the digital era. It will give prospective visitors an educational and aesthetically pleasing online platform that highlights the resort's breathtaking setting and features. The creation of the website is a visually captivating platform, featuring a user-friendly design, interactive elements, and an immersive gallery section for virtual tours. The goal is to attract potential guests but also foster a sense of community through an engaging feedback page, creating a lasting connection between visitors and the Satu-Hati Resort. The project encompasses approved design elements, content selection, and the seamless integration of informative sections, ensuring a compelling and informative digital experience for all visitors
